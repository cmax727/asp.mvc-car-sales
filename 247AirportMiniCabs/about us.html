About Us

Airport M iniCabs247 its a London based company. We are a professional private hire minicab company specialized in transfers to and from all London Airports. Our services are based on a prompt and reliable minicab service. We combine a top class service with highly competitive prices to ensure we remain your only choice when it comes to book airport transfers.

Our core is to make sure that we offer excellent customer service and give you a high value for money especially if you compare us to Heathrow Taxis, Gatwick Taxis, Stansted Taxis, Luton Taxis and other pre-booked airport transfers companies.

We choose very carefully our drivers because we value our customers.

Our Drivers Meet & Greet passengers in the airport arrivals hall.For airport pick ups we add £5 car park charges and free waiting time for 1 hour.

We use the best possible route in terms of mileage to calculate our prices.

Bookings can be made by phone or on our secure website. We offer 2 ways to pay either by cash or card. Very important we do not charge for paying by card.

We wish a pleasant journey with us.
